ABOUT THE PROJECT

Project Description


The Meta Rose Project is a collection of audio-visual artworks composed of 3D-based visuals and original soundtracks exploring the concept of life compared to a rose.


Roses have contradictory aspects, both having beautiful petals and thorns simultaneously. Life is like holding a beautiful rose with thorns, as it encompasses both positive and negative experiences, as well as states of joy and sorrow. There is no life without any challenge, just as every rose has its thorns. However, this reality does not change the fact that roses are beautiful. Similarly, your life is beautiful if you choose to see the beauty in it.


Each audio-visual artwork in the collection conveys the concept of inseparable mixed emotions, drawing parallels to roses and thorns, with each piece visually and sonically communicating with the others. Together, they form an interactive visual album for the audience.




The collection includes:


The Digital Twin of the Artist, “Mintie”

3D Rendered images and videos of Mintie

The Meta Rose Game_The Multiplayer Game built in Unreal Engine5

The Meta Rose Touch_Interactive audio-visual roses using Playtronica + Touchdesigner

Fabulous and Fearless_Music Video


EP Album with all tracks + Album Cover




Throughout the project, two contrasting visual symbols are employed:


- The first symbol represents positive emotions, energy, and life, depicted through the colour pink, rose petals, and flowing movements.


- The second symbol portrays non-positive emotions, challenges, and lifelessness, represented by the colour grey or chrome, thorns, and deep water.
